Casual Nights: New time and location

10/29/2014 by Isaac Lean

Now that we are an official organization, we now have a new venue for our Casual Night events at Baskin 330. This is to address the occupancy and sound limitations of our old venue at Oakes.

Casual Night, previously known as Fight Night, is an event for everyone to get together and just play some care-free matches in some fighting games. Everyone of any skill level and game are welcome, so don't hesitate to bring in some other games beyond the main three of Ultra Street Fighter 4, Ultimate Marvel vs. Capcom 3, and Super Smash Bros Melee.

We are always in need of TVs, consoles, and games, so if you are willing to bring any of them please do so!

Casual Nights happen every other week on Thursday from 8:00PM to 12:00AM, so feel free to stop by at anytime during this time block and play a few games... or even better, stay the whole time! For specific dates please check out Events & Schedule page.

New SCFGC Website: A work in progress

10/29/2014 by Isaac Lean

Welcome to the all new website for the Santa Cruz Fighting Game Community. This website is a way for us to centralize information about the community in an organized manner. It is also a way for us to keep community members who don't use any of our online communities up to date with community affairs.

Because we want the main content of the website up as soon as possible, it is now online but the entire site is still not 100% complete yet. Still everything should be mostly working, but if you find any bugs or issues please let us know!
