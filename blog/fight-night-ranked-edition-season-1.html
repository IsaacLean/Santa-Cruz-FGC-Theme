Fight Night: Ranked Edition - Season 1

11/05/2014 by Isaac Lean

The Santa Cruz Fighting Game Community is proud to present an all new event series: Fight Night: Ranked Edition.

At these events players will battle for points and climb their way up the rankings in a double-elimination tournament format. So far the ranked matches feature the following games:

Ultra Street Fighter IV
Super Smash Bros. Melee
Project M

At each event points are given to players who place in the top 13 ranks and the amount of points each player earns varies depending on how high their rank is. At the end of each week the points are calculated and players can see how they are progressing through the rankings found on this website. For each event you must participate in at least one bracket match to be eligible for points.

Super Smash Bros. Melee and Project M will alternate every other event, starting with Super Smash Bros. Melee on November 7 and Project M on November 21.

While normally lasting throughout one quarter at UCSC, Season 1 will instead last through both Fall and Winter quarter.

Fight Night: Ranked Edition happens every other week on Friday at Baskin 330 with sign-ups starting from 7:30 PM to 8:30 PM, so make sure you sign-up within this period otherwise you will not be able to compete! For specific dates please check out Events & Schedule page.

We are always in need of TVs, consoles, and games, so if you are willing to bring any of them please do so! Also setup starts at 7:30, so if you're feeling nice and want to help move things go ahead.

Ultra Street Fighter IV Rules

  • Game Settings: Versus Mode, 99 Seconds, 2/3 Rounds, Edition Select = Off, No Handicap
  • Grand finals, winners finals, and losers finals will be best of 5 per set. All other matches will be best of 3.
  • If the players do not agree on a stage within 15 seconds, the match will be random stage select.
  • Winner may change ultra only if opponent changes characters, but has to pick ultra first after loser picks character.

Super Smash Bros. Melee Rules

  • Game Settings: 4 Stock, 8 Minute Time Limit, No Items
  • Grand finals, winners finals, and losers finals will be best of 5 per set. All other matches will be best of 3.
  • Starter stages: Yoshiâ€™s Story, Fountain of Dreams, Final Destination, Battlefield, Dream Land
  • Additional counter-pick Stages: Pokemon Stadium
  • Wobbling is legal. Freezing and infinite stalling are not legal.
  • First stage is decided by banning from the Starter Stages in P1-P2-P2-P1 order.
  • Winning player is not locked into the same character for the next match, but has to pick before loser selects character.
  • Following stages decided by winner banning one stage from all stages and then loser choosing.
  • Any matches that best of 5, there are no stage bans.
  • You may not choose a stage you have already won on unless mutually agreed to.
