Fight Night: Casual Edition returns

10/29/2014 by Isaac Lean

Now that we are an official organization, we now have a new venue for our Fight Night: Casual Edition events at Baskin 330. This is to address the occupancy and sound limitations of our old venue at Oakes.

Fight Night: Casual Edition is an event for everyone to get together and just play some care-free matches in some fighting games. Everyone of any skill level and game is welcome, so don't hesitate to bring in some other games beyond the main three of Ultra Street Fighter 4, Ultimate Marvel vs. Capcom 3, and Super Smash Bros Melee.

We are always in need of TVs, consoles, and games, so if you are willing to bring any of them please do so! Also setup starts at 7:30, so if you're feeling nice and want to help move things come around this time.

Fight Night: Casual Edition happen every other week on Thursday from 8:00PM to 12:00AM, so feel free to stop by at anytime during this time block and play a few games! For specific dates please check out Events & Schedule page.
