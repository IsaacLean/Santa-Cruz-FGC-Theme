Location

Our main venue is located at Baskin 330 which is on the third floor of UCSC's Jack Baskin School of Engineering building. This room holds our Casual Night and Ranked Night events. For specific dates and times of these events please check the Events & Schedules page.

Other events will most likely be in a different location. Again, please check the Events & Schedules page for more details on other events.

Note that sometimes the location and time of events may change, so please check our Facebook group and stay updated on any event changes.

If you have any problems finding the location, again please don't hesitate to use our Facebook group and message us!
