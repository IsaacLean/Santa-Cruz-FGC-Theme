Location

Most of our events occur in two rooms. For Fight Night: Ranked Edition, we hold the events at Baskin E2 192 which is located on the first floor of UCSC's Jack Baskin School of Engineering 2 building. For Fight Night: Casual Edition we hold events at Baskin 379 which is located on the third floor of UCSC's Jack Baskin School of Engineering 1 building.

For specific dates and times of these events please check the Events & Schedules page.

Note that sometimes the location and time of events may change, so please check our Facebook group and stay updated on any event changes.

If you have any problems finding the location, again please don't hesitate to use our Facebook group and message us!
