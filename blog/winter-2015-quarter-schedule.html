Winter 2015 Quarter Schedule

01/08/2015 by Isaac Lean

This quarter our scheduled has changed! Now we are going to have one Ranked Fight Night and one Casual Fight Night every week. Please check out the new schedule here.

Ranked Fight Nights will be every Friday from 7:00 PM to 12:00 AM starting on January 16. The location has not been confirmed just yet, but we will announce it as soon as we know. There are also some other nice things about Ranked Fight Night now that will be revealed very soon, so please keep your eye on the blog and Facebook group for the latest updates.

Casual Fight Nights will be every Saturday from 7:00 PM to 11:30 PM in Baskin Engineering 379.

As for this Friday, we will have a special highlight event for Guilty Gear Xrd and Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo Wii U. This will basically be a Casual Fight Night with the focus on these new games, so all other fighting games are still welcome, but we would really like you to try these games out! The location will be either Baskin Engineering 330 or Baskin Engineering 379 (we'll know which specific room by Friday and let you guys know) from 7:00 PM to 12:00 AM.

As for upcoming tournaments...

  • 1/17: Gamerz Phunk Lab Tournament - Guilty Gear Xrd, Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo Wii U, Ultimate Marvel vs. Capcom 3, Ultra Street Fighter IV
  • 2/7: Level Up Video Games Ultra Street Fighter IV Tournament

Ultra Street Fighter IV Rules

  • Game Settings: Versus Mode, 99 Seconds, 2/3 Rounds, Edition Select = Off, No Handicap
  • Grand finals, winners finals, and losers finals will be best of 5 per set. All other matches will be best of 3.
  • If the players do not agree on a stage within 15 seconds, the match will be random stage select.
  • Winner may change ultra only if opponent changes characters, but has to pick ultra first after loser picks character.

Super Smash Bros. Melee Rules

  • Game Settings: 4 Stock, 8 Minute Time Limit, No Items
  • Grand finals, winners finals, and losers finals will be best of 5 per set. All other matches will be best of 3.
  • Starter stages: Yoshi’s Story, Fountain of Dreams, Final Destination, Battlefield, Dream Land
  • Additional counter-pick Stages: Pokemon Stadium
  • Wobbling is legal. Freezing and infinite stalling are not legal.
  • First stage is decided by banning from the Starter Stages in P1-P2-P2-P1 order.
  • Winning player is not locked into the same character for the next match, but has to pick before loser selects character.
  • Following stages decided by winner banning one stage from all stages and then loser choosing.
  • Any matches that best of 5, there are no stage bans.
  • You may not choose a stage you have already won on unless mutually agreed to.

Project M Rules

  • Game Settings: 4 Stock, 8 Minute Time Limit, No Items
  • Grand finals, winners finals, and losers finals will be best of 5 per set. All other matches will be best of 3.
  • Starter stages: Yoshi’s Story, Fountain of Dreams, Final Destination, Battlefield, Dream Land, Pokemon Stadium 2, Smashville
  • Additional counter-pick Stages: Yoshi's Island, WarioWare, Norfair, Skyworld, Green Hill Zone
  • Freezing and infinite stalling are not legal.
  • First stage is decided by banning from the Starter Stages in P1-P2-P1 order.
  • Winning player is not locked into the same character for the next match, but has to pick before loser selects character.
  • Following stages decided by winner banning three stages from all legal stages and then loser choosing from the remaining available stages.
  • Any matches that best of 5, there are no stage bans.
  • You may not choose a stage you have already won on unless mutually agreed to.
