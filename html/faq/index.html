1. What is the Santa Cruz Fighting Game Community?

The Santa Cruz Fighting Game Community is an organization registered under the University of California, Santa Cruz's SOAR. The organization's goal is to grow and maintain a community of fighting gamers within the Santa Cruz area.

Back to top

2. How do I become a member?

No need to fill out any forms. Just come to an event and play! If you would like feel free to also introduce yourself through a post on our online communities.

Back to top

3. What games are played?

The three games we mainly play are Ultra Street Fighter IV, Ultimate Marvel vs. Capcom 3, and Super Smash Bros. Melee. Even if you don't play these three games, we're very open to playing any fighting game so don't hesitate to ask if anyone wants to play another game through our online communities or bring the game to Fight Night: Casual Edition.

Back to top

4. When and where does the community play?

Time and location varies depending on the event, but for Fight Night: Casual Edition we meet every other week on Thursday from 8PM - 12AM at Baskin 330. Please check the Events & Schedule page for specific dates as well as information on other events.

Back to top

5. How can I connect with local players?

You can connect with local players by posting in our online communities and participating in events.

If you want to play games outside of events with people, just ask who wants to play in our online communities. This is what they are for and we strongly encourage players to do this!

7. Is there any way I can help out?

The Santa Cruz Fighting Game Community is a grassroots organization, so it's actually very easy to get involved with leadership responsibilities if you are an active member. If you have any interest with this topic please contact the people listed in the previous question either through our online communities or through Twitter.

For other ways to help out besides leadership roles, at every event we always need extra TVs, consoles, and games, so please bring them if you can. It's even better if you let an event coordinator know you are bringing things ahead of time!

Back to top

8. How can I add an event to the schedule?

Contact the people listed in question 6 either through our online communities or through Twitter.

We are always looking to add events to our calendar so we can get more players out to them, so don't hesitate to let us know about your events!
