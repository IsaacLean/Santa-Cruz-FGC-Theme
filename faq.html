What is the Santa Cruz Fighting Game Community?

The Santa Cruz Fighting Game Community is an organization registered under the University of California, Santa Cruz's SOAR. The organization's goal is to grow and maintain a community of fighting gamers within the Santa Cruz area.

Back to top

How do I become a member?

No need to fill out any forms. Just come to an event and play! If you would like feel free to also introduce yourself through a post on our online communities.

Back to top

What games are played?

The three games we mainly play are Ultra Street Fighter IV, Ultimate Marvel vs. Capcom 3, and Super Smash Bros. Melee. Even if you don't play these three games, we're very open to playing any fighting game so don't hesitate to ask if anyone wants to play another game through our online communities or bring the game to Casual Night.

Back to top

When and where does the community play?

Time and location varies depending on the event, but for Casual Night we meet every other week on Thursday from 8PM - 12AM at Baskin 330. Please check the Events & Schedule page for more information.

Back to top

How can I connect with local players?

You can connect with local players by posting in our online communities and participating in events.

Back to top

How can I play games with the community outside of events?

Just ask who wants to play in our online communities. This is what they are for, and we strongly encourage players to do this!
